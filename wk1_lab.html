Cruises

The Eastern Travel Express Line offers a series of ships built specifically to travel the rivers of Southeast Asia in a style that is consistent with colonial times and traditions. The ships are small, having three decks and accommodating 65 passengers in 200 sq. ft. staterooms.
Staterooms are equiped with A/C, private bathrooms with shower and hair dryers.
